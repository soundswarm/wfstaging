American Pilsner

WarFlag's American Pilsner was created to bring back the amazing American flavor lost over the last century. We poured hundreds of recipe variations to bring you what we think is the most flavorful American style pilsner in the country. Our Pilsner begins with sweet honey characters accented with spicy and grassy hop notes. With each sip you'll experience a smooth, yet dry finish that captures the essence of an easy drinking beer. We raise our glass with you.

FIGHT THE GOOD FIGHT

Our goal is to have a positive impact on the community. We want to eliminate the abuse of power through education. War Flag will be making annual donations to The Bill of Rights Institute helping them further their mission.

The Institute is a 501(c)(3) not for profit charity focused on providing educational resources on America’s Founding documents and principles for teachers and students of American History and Civics.

Mission

  The mission of the Bill of Rights Institute is to educate young people about the words and ideas of America’s Founders, the liberties guaranteed in our Founding documents, and how our Founding principles continue to affect and shape a free society. It is the goal of the Institute to help the next generation understand the freedom and opportunity the Constitution offers.

Vision

The vision of the Institute is to create a citizenry that has the knowledge, values, dispositions, and skills to exercise the rights and responsibilities needed to maintain a free society.
