Our story

We were inspired to start a craft beer company for two reasons. We’re passionate about GREAT BEER and were passionate about AMERICA.

By "great beer," we mean:  A beer you can truly appreciate because you know each ingredient is specifically selected to harmonize together to create an outstanding liquid. A beer that makes you want to savor each sip!

  We are living in the renaissance of craft beer, so we wanted to return to our brewing roots and help revive this American pastime.

We're also equally passionate about America, and we feel there needs to be a larger conversation about Americans’ civil liberties. We hope WarFlag gets Americans talking more about their amendment rights and how they need to be upheld. WarFlag's image of a snake, clad in the American flag’s original 13 stars while coiled around the wooden barrel that ferments  beer is the perfect embodiment of our two passions.

Supporting Civil Liberties

We are excited about donating a portion of our sales to the Bill of Rights Institute , a 501(c)(3) not for profit charity focused on providing educational resources on America’s Founding documents and principles for teachers and students of American History and Civics, so that we can help make a positive impact in schools

Our Beer

American Pilsner

Our Classic American Pilsner is a hopped up tribute to an earlier time and we're really proud of our finished product. It starts with sweet honey characters that are accented with spicy and grassy hop notes with a smooth yet dry finish that truly rounds out the beer.

The Brewery

Our original recipes are brewed at Ipswich Ale House by Brooklyn brew master, Peter Salmond. Peter started his career over 15 years ago with Sacramento Brewing Company and his beer has won numerous awards including bronze medals at the Great American Beer Festival and the World Beer Cup.
